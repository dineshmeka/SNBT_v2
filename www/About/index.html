ABOUT US

Smt Nandamuri Basava Tarakuri Memorial Degree College for Women(SNBT) has been established in the year 1989 under the dynamic leadership of Janab.Khadar Shrief . The institution is sponsored by Madina Educational Society. Shri Janab.Khadar Shrief garu, our founder Chairman has been in the field of education industry for the last 25 years with an intention of spreading quality education among children at the school level. He established his first technical college in the year 2002 and over a span of 25 years he established professional colleges first womens college. which is in the jurisdiction of Repalle,Bapatla Dist Andhra Pradesh State in the year 1989.
